Pickle Dip

Ingredients

  • Sour Cream
  • Cream Cheese
  • Dill Pickles
  • Worcestershire Sauce
  • Garlic Powder
  • Dill Weed
  • Pickle Juice

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Mix sour cream, cream cheese, pickles, Worcestershire sauce, garlic powder, and dill together in a bowl until well combined.
  3. Stir in pickle juice to taste.
  4. Serve with potato chips and enjoy.
