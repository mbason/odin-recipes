Bacon and Dill Pickle Cheese Ball

Ingredients

  • Cream cheese
  • Sour cream
  • Dill pickles
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Jack cheese
  • Pickle juice
  • Green onion
  • Paprika
  • Dried dill
  • Pretzels
  • Bacon crumbles

Directions

  1. Combine cream cheese, sour cream, diced dill pickles, Cheddar cheese, Monterey Jack cheese, dill pickle juice, green onions, paprika, and dried dill in a large bowl.
    Stir until evenly combined. Form mixture into a ball. Cover and refrigerate for a minimum of 1 hour.
  2. Just before serving, place pretzels in a food processor and pulse until bread crumb consistency. Combine pretzel crumbs and bacon on a flat plate.
  3. Roll the cheese ball in the pretzel mixture until all sides are coated.
